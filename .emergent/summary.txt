The user is developing AchaServiço, a mobile-first application for local services using React Native/Expo and FastAPI. The initial strategy pivoted from immediate monetization to a growth-focused phase.

The primary goal is to launch the app on the Google Play Store as a completely **free service** to attract a critical mass of 100+ service providers. After a growth period, a tiered subscription model will be introduced.

- **Core App**: A React Native/Expo and FastAPI application. The app must be 100% free for all users and providers, with all payment/subscription-related UI and logic hidden or removed.
- **Provider Features**:

    - A Verified badge (⭐) should be automatically awarded to providers who receive 5+ positive reviews (4+ stars).

    - Providers should be able to select multiple service photos (up to 6) at once.

    - Users should be able to favorite providers and view them in a list on their profile.
    - Users should be able to share a provider's profile via WhatsApp, etc.
- **Admin Features**:
    - An admin panel with statistics and user/provider management.
    - Ability to filter users and providers by name.
    - Ability to send a broadcast push notification to all providers.
- **Future Phase (Monetization)**:
    - Implement a 3-tier subscription model as detailed in .
